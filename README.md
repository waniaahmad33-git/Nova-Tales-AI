# 📖 Nova Tales AI

### An AI-Powered Interactive Storytelling Platform

Nova Tales AI is an innovative interactive novel platform that transforms traditional reading into a fully immersive AI-powered experience.

Readers can read stories, listen to narrated chapters, interact with AI characters, control the platform through voice commands, and even share feedback directly with the writer.

Built around the novel "Pehchan Ka Sawal", the platform combines storytelling, artificial intelligence, voice technology, and user interaction into one seamless experience.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Traditional novels offer limited interaction between readers and stories.

Nova Tales AI solves this problem by creating an intelligent storytelling environment where readers become active participants instead of passive readers.

The platform introduces AI-driven conversations, voice narration, character interactions, and real-time engagement features that make stories more immersive and enjoyable.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

Traditional digital novels often lack:

• Reader engagement

• Interactive experiences

• Multimedia support

• Personalized storytelling

• Real-time communication with characters

• Direct communication between readers and writers

Modern readers seek dynamic experiences that combine storytelling with AI and voice technologies.

## ✨ Key Features

### 📚 Interactive Story Reading

Read story chapters with a modern and visually appealing interface.

### 🎨 AI Generated Scene Illustrations

Every scene is supported with relevant visuals to improve immersion.

### 🔊 Text-to-Speech Narration

Listen to chapters through realistic voice narration.

### 🎙 Voice Command Support

Control reading using commands such as:

• Start Reading

• Stop Reading

• Read From This Line

### 🤖 AI Character Chatbots

Chat with story characters as if they were real people.

### 📞 Character Calling System

Initiate voice calls with your favorite story characters.

### 💬 Real-Time Conversations

Experience natural AI-powered interactions.

### 📝 Suggestion & Feedback Portal

Share ideas, reviews, and suggestions with the writer.

### 👨‍💻 Direct Writer Feedback System

Create a stronger connection between readers and authors.
